Below are pictures I have taken as I practice becoming a better amateaur photographer and photographer assistant to help support Kevin Weitzner's, my significant other, aspiration to become a professional photographer.

Still Image

  • I took this picture of a yellow flower during my vacation in the Philippines in 2010. This is one of my first attempts at photography. I am particularly proud of this picture because I focused properly on the flower. Although I am not that knowledgeable about photography, I know it came out right because the flower is in focus and the background is blurry. Kevin did the appopriate editing using Adobe Lightroom. I took this picture using a Canon EOS Digital Rebel XS with a Tamron 17-50mm 2.8.

Action Shot

  • One of Kevin's first gigs as a photographer was taking pictures for the Army. I thought it would be a good idea to take pictures of him in action so that propspective clients could get a taste for how he works in the field. This is a picture of him trying to positioning the soldiers so that their group pictures would have better framing. I used a Fujifilm FinePix X100.
