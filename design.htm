Design Portfolio

Below are examples of documents I have designed for academic credit. I have additional work experience designing a wide variety of other documents ranging from brochures and guides to name badges and tent cards.

Process Explanation

  • The purpose of this assignment was to explain a process using a combination of text and pictures to help the audience understand it. I chose to describe allergies. I designed the document as if my audience consisted of people who suffered from allergies and wanted to understand the process of having a reaction. I used Microsoft Publisher to create this document.

Newsletter

  • This newsletter was created collaboratively using Adobe InDesign for a group project in Document Design at Illinois Institute of Technology (IIT). We had to make sure our newsletter met IIT Branding Style guidelines while appealing to the targeted audience, Computer Science alumni. Our group used Robin Williams' four basic design principles (proximity, alignment, repeitition, and contrast) to guide our design choices. Color was used significantly to group similar information. We used Adobe InDesign to create this document.
